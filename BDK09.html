Publication and Peer Review

Description

Overview of the process of publication of scientific data and information, including peer review, journal literature, and limitations of the scientific literature

Objectives

At the completion of this component, the learner will be able to:

  • Describe the steps in the life cycle of scientific information
  • Discuss scientific peer review and its limitations
  • Describe limitations of the scientific literature
