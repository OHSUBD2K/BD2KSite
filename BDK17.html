Choice of Algorithms and Algorithm Dynamics

Description

Overview of algorithms, considerations for choice of algorithms and evaluating algorithmic dynamics.

Objectives

At the completion of this component, the learner will be able to:

  • Define basic concepts with respect to algorithm design and evaluation.
  • Describe the factors that influence choice of algorithms and the impact on interpretation and performance.
  • Describe the basic processes of evaluating algorithm dynamics.
