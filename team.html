Meet the team

This is a group of people dedicated to providing the highest quality of educational materials about "Big Data."

William Hersh, MD, FACMI, FACP

William (Bill) Hersh is Professor and Chair of the Department of Medical Informatics & Clinical Epidemiology Oregon Health & Science University. Dr. Hersh is a leader and innovator in biomedical informatics both in education and research. Bill is a co-PI on this project.
