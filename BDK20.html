Regulatory Issues in Big Data for Genomics and Health

Description

Research regulations; Privacy regulations; Compliance

Objectives

At the completion of this component, the learner will be able to:

  • Define and describe issues in privacy, confidentiality, and security of clinical information
  • Discuss the HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules
